Multi-root trust architecture providing mathematically independent assurance paths for AI systems in safety-critical applications. Addresses single-point-of-failure risks in conventional safety frameworks through coprime factorization.

**Status**: Published specification | Formal verification tooling in development

**Key Innovation**: Compositional entropy budgets enabling formal reasoning about AI system behavior  
**Status**: Published framework | Industrial case studies in progress

## Research Approach

Our frameworks prioritize:
- **Mathematical rigor**: Formal proofs and compositional guarantees
- **Industrial applicability**: Designed for real-world constraints and deployment
- **Quantitative assurance**: Measurable safety and security properties
- **Independence principles**: Eliminating correlation risks through formal methods
